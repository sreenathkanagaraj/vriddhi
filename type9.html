International Textile Trade Organization

About Us

The International Textile Trade Organization is a global network of textile manufacturers, retailers, and designers dedicated to fostering international trade and cooperation in the textile industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the growth and development of the textile industry worldwide. We aim to foster innovation, sustainability, and fair trade practices in the textile industry.

Our Services

Trade Facilitation

We facilitate international trade in textiles by providing a platform for networking, negotiation, and collaboration.

Policy Advocacy

We advocate for policies that promote fair trade, sustainability, and growth in the textile industry.

Market Research

We provide our members with valuable market research and insights to help them stay competitive in the global market.

About Us

We are a global textile trade organization committed to promoting the growth and sustainability of the textile industry. We provide a platform for networking, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas and knowledge among textile professionals worldwide.

Our Services

Networking

We provide opportunities for members to connect and collaborate with textile industry professionals from around the world.

Education

We offer a range of educational resources and programs to help our members stay informed about the latest trends and developments in the textile industry.

Advocacy

We advocate for the interests of the textile industry at the local, national, and international levels, and work to promote policies that support the growth and sustainability of the industry.
